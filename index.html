About

I am a psychiatric resident and postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Biological Psychiatry. My research focuses on psychiatric genetics, using Danish health registers and biobank data to develop methods for genetic risk assessment.

I work with polygenic scores, family-based genetic risk approaches, and large-scale registry studies to better understand the genetic architecture of psychiatric disorders.

Research

Psychiatric genetics — polygenic scores, family-based genetic risk, and registry-based studies.
